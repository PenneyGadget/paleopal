How Does it Work?

1. Easily sign in with your Facebook account

2. Click "Create First Entry" from your dashboard

3. Enter the date, the meal, the ingredients, and
any other details you would like to remember

4. Click "Calculate" to see your macronutrient
intake for that meal or "Save" to save the
entry to your journal

5. Visit your dashboard at any time to see a
running list of all your saved days and meals

6. Visit "Resources" to see guidelines, recipes
shopping tips and more!!
